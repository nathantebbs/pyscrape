PyScrape Test Webpage

Hello, this webpage is a testing utility for the PyScrape CLI

Why use PyScrape?

PyScrape is designed for getting downloadable content from simple websites

The idea for this tool came from spending, granted a small fraction of time, going through various webpages, related to school or other pursuits. and needing to download their linked content

What websites does the CLI work on?

PyScrape works best on websites that look a lot like this!

Using webscraping we are able to programatically target links that contain specific types of content like pdfs, by filtering through file extensions.

This means that if you see content like this:

Author Title Released Download
Research Science Institute Staff Introduction to LaTeX 2015 PDF
Gareth James, et al. An Introduction to Statistical Learning 2023 PDF
Joseph C. Watkins An Introduction to the Science of Statistics: From Theory to Implementation Preliminary Edition PDF
Arian Maleki and Tom Do Review of Probability Theory n.d. PDF

In the past, I have gone through individual items for a given web page that looked a lot like this one. However, I made a key mistake. I should've just downloaded all the content related to that class, in case I might need it later!

Some may call this hording, I call it archiving. Either way, sorting a websites content and downloading all it has to offer is the inteded use of this tool, enjoy!
